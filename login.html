What We Do

Wildlife-friendly farming practices: This involves promoting farming practices that are compatible with wildlife conservation, such as using natural pest control methods and preserving habitat features like hedgerows and wetlands.

Wildlife corridors and connectivity: This involves creating and maintaining corridors or connections between natural habitats to allow wildlife to move and disperse between populations, reducing the risk of isolation and genetic bottlenecking.
